About

Milk Matters is facilitated by International Board Certified Lactation Consultants, Ann Marie Neeley and MaryAnn Martin. Our lactation consultants offer free, evidence-based, clinical breastfeeding support and weight checks, to all families in our community.

We know that breastfeeding looks different for every parent and we respect you and the choices you make for your unique family. We will meet you where you are in your breastfeeding and parenting journey, with judgment-free help.

Mission

The mission of Flourish is to connect the community to free, high-quality breastfeeding support and education. ​

Vision

Flourish envisions an increase in breastfeeding rates to reduce racial, economic and health disparities while promoting the well-being of families and the community.
