Schedule

Milk Matters provides weekday morning programs the 2nd and 4th Wednesdays of each month, weekday evening programs on the 3rd Wednesday of each month and weekend afternoon programs the 1st Saturday of each month (except January).

Weekday morning gatherings are held at Banneker Community Center Playroom on the 3rd Floor. Banneker Community Center is located at 930 W. 7th Street, at the NE corner of 7th & N. Elm. Street parking available or there is a lot 1.5 blocks north at 9th & Elm. It is also accessible by Bloomington Transit #3 bus line to 5th & Elm, 2 blocks. Morning programs run from 9:30am to 11:30am.

Weekday evening gatherings are held at City Hall in McCloskey Room 135. City Hall is located at 401 N. Morton Street. There is free, lighted parking in lot. It is accessible by Bloomington Transit #2W bus line to Showers Complex. Evening programs run from 6:30pm to 8pm.

Weekend afternoon gatherings are held at the Crestmont Boys and Girls Club Teen Room on the Mezzanine level. Crestmont Boys & Girls Club is located at 1111 W 12th St, Bloomington, on the SW corner of Monroe and 12th. Free street parking is available. It is accessible by Bloomington Transit’s #2W bus line to the 13th and Summit stop (request closest stop at corner of 12th/Monroe). Saturday afternoon programs run from 2:00pm to 4:00pm.

Melissa Larimer of Flora Massage and Wellness Services will be volunteering with Milk Matters, providing free TummyTime!™ classes during the Saturday afternoon meetings at Crestmont Boys & Girls Club starting in March! Tummy time helps babies soften tissue, strengthen muscles and support overall development, helping to optimize feeding, digestion and comfort. While working with Melissa, parents can expect to help their baby get into tummy time comfortably and learn infant massage techniques.

About

Milk Matters is facilitated by International Board Certified Lactation Consultants, Ann Marie Neeley, MaryAnn Martin and Sarah Blankenbaker. Our lactation consultants offer free, evidence-based, clinical breastfeeding support and weight checks, to all families in our community. You can find more information about our lactation consultants here.

We know that breastfeeding looks different for every parent and we respect you and the choices you make for your unique family. We will meet you where you are in your breastfeeding and parenting journey, with judgment-free help. You can find more information on the Flourish Inc. board here

Mission and Vision

Mission

The mission of Flourish is to connect the community to free, high-quality breastfeeding support and education.

Vision

Flourish envisions an increase in breastfeeding rates to reduce racial, economic and health disparities while promoting the well-being of families and the community.
