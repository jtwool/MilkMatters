Milk Matters in-person clinics are suspended indefinitely due to the corona virus outbreak.
Good news is we are now available to help answer your questions on any day!
Please send us a private message on Facebook @milkmattersbloomington or by email to milkmattersbloomington@gmail.com.
Our lactation consultants will reply and arrange to provide you with support and information using email, phone, or video with FaceTime or Google Hangouts.
As always, all services provided by Milk Matters are 100% free.

Milk Matters' Lactation Consultants

Ann Marie Neeley, IBCLC

Ann Marie is a board certified lactation consultant who has been supporting families in Bloomington, Indiana with breastfeeding since 2002. She currently works in the lactation departments of St. Vincent Women’s Hospital and IU Health Bloomington Hospital. In addition, she is co-founder of Flourish, a non-profit organization that provides free breastfeeding support in Bloomington in the form of a drop-in clinic. Her areas of interest include working with parents who have used opioids, infant tongue-tie, supporting employed parents, and perinatal mood disorders. She currently serves as President of the Monroe County Breastfeeding Coalition.

MaryAnn Martin, Ph.D., IBCLC

MaryAnn was first trained as a cell biologist, a career that brought her to Bloomington, IN in 1995. Motherhood led to her second career as a lactation consultant – blending her scientific training, fascination with the biology of breastfeeding, and enthusiasm to learn directly from mothers and babies. MaryAnn has been a La Leche League Leader since 2005 and a board certified lactation consultant since 2011. In 2015 she co-founded Flourish, a non-profit organization that provides free breastfeeding support for families in Monroe and surrounding counties. MaryAnn is the Secretary/Treasurer of the Monroe County Breastfeeding Coalition.

Sarah Blankenbaker, RN, IBCLC

Sarah is a registered nurse and board certificated lactation consultant. She has lived in Bloomington since 2006 and has been supporting families with new babies in this community since 2012. She works at IU Health Bloomington Hospital providing lactation support and teaching community breastfeeding classes. She has training in yoga, massage therapy, and herbalism. Sarah is passionate about living a healthy lifestyle and creating a strong community that supports and empowers women.

The Flourish Inc. Board of Directors 2020-2021

Mary Ann Martin, Ph.D., IBCLC, President

MaryAnn was first trained as a cell biologist, a career that brought her to Bloomington, IN in 1995. Motherhood led to her second career as a lactation consultant – blending her scientific training, fascination with the biology of breastfeeding, and enthusiasm to learn directly from parents and babies. MaryAnn has been a La Leche League Leader since 2005, an International Board Certified Lactation Consultant since 2011, the Secretary/Treasurer of the Monroe County Breastfeeding Coalition since 2015 and is a co-founder of Flourish, Inc.

Lydia Hamilton, MPH, CLS, Treasuruer

Lydia was born and raised in Bloomington. She is currently a health behavior doctoral candidate at Indiana University where she conducts qualitative research. Her current research is focused on human milk banks. She is a certified lactation specialist (CLS) and aspires to become an International Board Certified Lactation Consultant (IBCLC). In her spare time, she watches too much Netflix, plays with her cats, and volunteers for Milk Matters.

Meagan Niese, CFRE, Secretary

Meagan Niese is the Development Director of the Community Foundation of Bloomington and Monroe County, leading the Foundation’s efforts in fundraising and donor services. She has more than 10 years of nonprofit fundraising and marketing experience. She has called Bloomington home since 2003. Meagan became passionate about advocating for women and lactation support when she became a mom in 2013 to her daughter, and then to twin boys in 2015. Having walked through the journey and challenges of being a new parent, she enjoys giving back to support other families through serving on the board of Flourish, Inc.

Katharine Esarey, LCSW , Board Member

Katharine is a licensed clinical social worker who has worked with individuals and families in Bloomington since 2011. She is a medical social worker at IU Health Bloomington Hospital and works with women and families in the perinatal period to ensure they have access to community resources and support. Kathryn Knecht, RDN, CD, CLS, Board Member

Kathryn is a registered dietitian, a certified lactation specialist and former IBCLC. She has been supporting families in our community since 1992 through her work at the WIC Program. She is also a member of the Indiana WIC Breastfeeding Committee. Through her work on that committee, she helps to plan and implement the breastfeeding support provided by the Indiana WIC Program.

Julie Marsh , Board Member

Julie was born and raised in Bloomington and is now raising her two children here, as well. Julie found an online lactation support group when she had her second child that provided her with supportive information and other parents who shared similar experiences. Over the years, she began to offer support to new members of that online group. Julie joined the Flourish board because she wanted to give back to people in her local community too and to make sure others had access to the support she had received from local lactation consultants with both her children. Julie enjoys reading, crafting and spending time with her family.

Meghann Goetz , Board Member

Meghann has been an elementary school teacher in Bloomington since 2014. This work is grounded in her belief in helping all children and families. In 2015, she became a mother and embarked on a journey of breastfeeding struggles. Luckily, she was able to find a supportive community to help feed her son. When she had her daughter in 2017, Milk Matters was available from day one to help. Meghann believes that this support for families in our community should start from the very beginning and be available to all.
